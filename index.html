What we Do

Transforming Boring Classrooms Into FUN SIMULATIVE PLAYROOMS

Problem

Education system is unable to solve the purpose ,it was meant for. Students are attending classes either for marks or attendance thus missing out on real world knowldege and skill.

Analysis
